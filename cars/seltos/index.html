Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the top model for Kia Seltos, and what is its price?

Kia Seltos X Line MSRP* starts at Rs 18.29 Lakh (exclusive of all taxes).

How many airbags are included in the Kia Seltos?

Kia Seltos offers 6 airbags as standard across all variants.

What are the transmission options available in Kia Seltos?

The Kia Seltos is available in both manual and automatic transmissions, namely - 7DCT, 6AT, 6 MT, IVT/iMT. Choose from your preferences for the best driving experience.

Does Seltos come with a sunroof?

Yes, an electric sunroof is offered across all 5 variants of Kia Seltos car.
