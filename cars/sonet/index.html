Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the top model for Kia Sonet? And what is its price?

Kia Sonet MSRP* for the top model starts at Rs. 12.59 Lakh (exclusive of all taxes).

Does the Kia Sonet come with a sunroof?

Yes, electric sunroof is offered in Kia Sonet but in selected variants.

How many airbags does the Kia Sonet offer?

The all-new Kia Soner offers 4 airbags as standard and an option of 6 airbags in selected variants.

What options for transmission does the Kia Sonet offer?

The Kia Sonet is available in both manual and automatic transmissions, namely - 7DCT, 6AT, iMT and 5 MT.
